People who love speed know that it is not just an experience, it is life itself. And this is the place where we live that life. Speed - it inspires us, motivates us and it drives us to achieve the ‘Unthinkable’ and ‘Impossible’.

Team Vector is a team of engineering students who aims at the technical and managerial development of future of go karting.

We are a constantly learning and evolving Go Kart racing team from Presidency University, Bangalore-64, who aims to create a team with absolute Team Spirit and Leadership quality.

Team Vector has actively participated in NEKC 2.0 (National Electric Kart Championship 2.0) and secured the 16th position out of 120 and secured 8th position out of 123 in the NEKC 3.0 (National Electric Kart Championship3.0).

Our enthusiastic team members have passion for the sport and desire to learn, not only about driving but also the engineering side and vehicle dynamics.We whole heartedly welcome you to become a part of our team and experience a world sum of thrill and adventure.
